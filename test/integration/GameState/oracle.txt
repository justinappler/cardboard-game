: Options:
# of card to select
r to refresh screen
t for three more
n to play a new game (Select this option to change difficulty or game type)
p to pause
u to unpause game
h to recieve a hint
o to redisplay options
q to quit
These options only work for multiplayer mode
! for player 1 to buzz in
@ for player 2 to buzz in
# for player 3 to buzz in
$ for player 4 to buzz in
